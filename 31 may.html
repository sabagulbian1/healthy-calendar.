World No Tobacco Day - May 31st

World No Tobacco Day is observed annually on May 31st. It's a day dedicated to raising awareness about the health risks associated with tobacco use and advocating for effective policies to reduce tobacco consumption.

Tobacco use is one of the leading causes of preventable death worldwide, causing millions of deaths each year from diseases such as cancer, heart disease, and respiratory illnesses. World No Tobacco Day aims to educate people about the dangers of tobacco use and encourage them to quit smoking.
