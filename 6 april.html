International Day of Sport for Development and Peace - April 6th

The International Day of Sport for Development and Peace is observed annually on April 6th. It's a day dedicated to recognizing the power of sport as a tool for promoting peace, social inclusion, and sustainable development.

Sport has the ability to transcend boundaries, foster mutual understanding, and promote teamwork and respect. On this day, organizations, communities, and individuals around the world come together to celebrate the positive impact of sport on individuals and societies.
