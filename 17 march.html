World Sleep Day - March 17th

World Sleep Day is observed annually on March 17th. It's a day dedicated to raising awareness about the importance of healthy sleep and addressing sleep-related issues.

Quality sleep is essential for overall health and well-being. It plays a crucial role in physical and mental health, cognitive function, and daytime performance. World Sleep Day aims to emphasize the significance of adopting good sleep habits and seeking solutions for sleep disorders.
