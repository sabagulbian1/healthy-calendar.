International Day Against Drug Abuse and Illicit Trafficking - June 26th

The International Day Against Drug Abuse and Illicit Trafficking is observed annually on June 26th. It's a day dedicated to raising awareness about the global issue of drug abuse and the illicit trafficking of drugs.

Drug abuse and illicit trafficking have devastating effects on individuals, families, and communities worldwide. This day serves as a reminder of the importance of prevention, treatment, and rehabilitation efforts to address the drug problem.
