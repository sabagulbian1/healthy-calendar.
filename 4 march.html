World Obesity Day - March 4th

World Obesity Day is observed annually on March 4th. It's a day dedicated to raising awareness about the global obesity epidemic and promoting actions to prevent and manage obesity.

Obesity is a complex condition with serious health implications, including an increased risk of chronic diseases such as heart disease, diabetes, and certain cancers. World Obesity Day aims to address the social, environmental, and economic factors contributing to obesity and advocate for policies and interventions to support healthy lifestyles.
