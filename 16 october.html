World Fruit & Vegetables Day - October 16th

World Fruit & Vegetables Day is observed annually on October 16th. It's a day dedicated to promoting the consumption of fruits and vegetables for a healthy and balanced diet.

Fruits and vegetables are rich in vitamins, minerals, fiber, and antioxidants that are essential for maintaining good health and preventing chronic diseases. Incorporating a variety of fruits and vegetables into your diet can help reduce the risk of obesity, heart disease, diabetes, and other health conditions.
