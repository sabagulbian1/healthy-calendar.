World Alcohol-Free Day - October 3rd

World Alcohol-Free Day is observed annually on October 3rd. It's a day dedicated to promoting a healthy lifestyle and raising awareness about the benefits of abstaining from alcohol.

Alcohol-Free Day encourages individuals to take a break from alcohol consumption and reflect on its impact on their health and well-being. It's an opportunity to celebrate sobriety and support those who are striving for a life free from alcohol dependence.
