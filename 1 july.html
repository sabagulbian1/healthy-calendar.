International Fruit Day - July 1st

International Fruit Day is observed annually on July 1st. It's a day dedicated to celebrating the nutritional value, diversity, and deliciousness of fruits.

Fruits are rich in vitamins, minerals, fiber, and antioxidants that are essential for maintaining good health and preventing diseases. International Fruit Day encourages people to incorporate more fruits into their diet and explore the wide variety of fruits available.
